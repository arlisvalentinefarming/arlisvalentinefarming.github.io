About Us

Rooted in Sanger,
Focused on Excellence.

Arlis Valentine Farming, LLC represents the intersection of agricultural heritage and modern innovation. Located in the heart of Sanger, California, we specialize in "Other Crop Farming," dedicating our resources to cultivating high-value, specialized crops that thrive in our region's specific microclimates.

Our philosophy goes beyond simple production; we believe in stewardship of the land. By integrating sustainable practices with data-driven decision-making, we ensure that every acre produces its maximum potential without compromising soil health.

We partner directly with distributors and processors who value consistency and quality above all else. Our team is comprised of seasoned agronomy experts who understand the nuances of the Central Valley's agricultural landscape.

Whether dealing with market fluctuations or climate challenges, our adaptability is our greatest asset. At Arlis Valentine Farming, we don't just grow crops; we cultivate lasting partnerships built on reliability and superior yield quality.

Our Methodology

From Analysis to
Harvest.

Our process is cyclical and data-centric. We don't just plant seeds; we execute a calculated plan designed for optimal output.

1

Feasibility & Soil Analysis

Before a single seed is sown, we conduct rigorous testing of soil pH, nutrient density, and water retention to select the ideal crop varieties for the specific plot.

2

Active Cultivation

Utilizing integrated pest management and precision irrigation, we monitor crop development daily. This hands-on approach minimizes chemical inputs while maximizing health.

3

Harvest & Review

Harvesting is timed to the hour for optimal maturity. Post-harvest, we analyze yield data to refine our strategies for the subsequent growing season.
